Job Portal — URL Reference

Quick list of routes, their names, and which role typically uses them.

PathNameRoleNotes
/homePublicHomepage
/users/register/registerPublicRegister (choose employer/jobseeker)
/users/login/loginPublicLogin
/users/logout/logoutAuthenticatedLogout
/jobs/job_listPublic / JobseekerBrowse jobs
/jobs/dashboard/employer_dashboardEmployerView your jobs and applicants
/jobs/company/company_profileEmployerManage company profile
/jobs/create/create_jobEmployerCreate job posting
/applications/apply/<job_id>/apply_jobJobseekerApply to a job (text resume + optional file)
/applications/my/my_applicationsJobseekerList your applications (view/edit/delete)
/applications/detail/<app_id>/application_detailApplicant / EmployerView full application and resume
/applications/edit/<app_id>/edit_applicationJobseekerEdit/replace resume and message
/applications/delete/<app_id>/delete_applicationJobseekerDelete your application (removes file)
/applications/view/<job_id>/view_applicantsEmployerList applicants for a job
/applications/update/<app_id>/update_statusEmployerUpdate application status (pending/accepted/rejected)

File uploads are saved to /media/resumes/ in development and require MIME types PDF/DOC/DOCX/TXT and size between 2–5 MB.
